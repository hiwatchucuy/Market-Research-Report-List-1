<p><p>The rotary air compressor market is primarily divided into two types: oiled and oil-free rotary air compressors. Oiled rotary air compressors utilize oil for lubrication, enhancing efficiency and durability while often producing higher discharge temperatures and requiring regular maintenance. In contrast, oil-free rotary air compressors operate without oil, ensuring cleaner air output, making them ideal for applications in sensitive industries like pharmaceuticals and food processing. The choice between these types depends on the specific needs for air quality and operational efficiency.</p></p>
