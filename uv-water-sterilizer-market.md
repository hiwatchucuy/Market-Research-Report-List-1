<p><p>The UV water sterilizer market is categorized into low-pressure, medium-pressure, and high-pressure systems, each serving distinct applications. Low-pressure systems operate at lower wavelengths, effectively inactivating microorganisms but may require longer exposure times. Medium-pressure systems emit a broader spectrum of UV light, allowing for faster sterilization and handling larger volumes, making them suitable for industrial use. High-pressure systems offer high-intensity UV light for rapid disinfection, typically used in advanced water treatment facilities and applications requiring stringent pathogen control.</p></p>
