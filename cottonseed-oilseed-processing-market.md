<p><p>The cottonseed oilseed processing market is primarily divided into mechanical and chemical processing methods. Mechanical processing involves physical extraction techniques, such as pressing, to separate oil from seeds, preserving more natural properties. In contrast, chemical processing utilizes solvents, usually hexane, to efficiently extract oil, yielding higher quantities but often requiring further refining to remove residues. Each method caters to different market demands, balancing factors like extraction efficiency, oil quality, and production costs, ultimately impacting retail offerings and consumer preferences.</p></p>
