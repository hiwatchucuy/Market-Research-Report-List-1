<p><p>The Inconel alloy flange market features various types tailored for diverse applications. Integral flanges are forged as a single piece with pipes, ensuring strength and durability. Threaded flanges use internal threads for easy assembly and disassembly. Butt welding flanges are welded directly to the piping, providing a strong joint. Loose flanges allow for easy maintenance by enabling disassembly. Other types may include blind flanges and lap joint flanges, catering to specific engineering needs across industries.</p></p>
