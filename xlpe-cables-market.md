<p><p>The XLPE cables market is divided into four main types based on voltage ratings. LV XLPE cables (Low Voltage) are used for residential and commercial applications. MV XLPE cables (Medium Voltage) cater to utility and industrial sectors, facilitating reliable power distribution. HV XLPE cables (High Voltage) are designed for transmission over long distances, ensuring minimal energy loss. EHV XLPE cables (Extra High Voltage) are utilized in large-scale transmission networks, supporting grid stability and accommodating high-demand electricity flow efficiently.</p></p>
