<p><ul><li>Standalone Imaging System</li><li>Multimodal Imaging System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Pre-Clinical Imaging System market is primarily categorized into two types: Standalone Imaging Systems and Multimodal Imaging Systems. Standalone Imaging Systems focus on a single imaging technique, providing high-resolution images for specific applications such as MRI or PET scans. In contrast, Multimodal Imaging Systems integrate multiple imaging modalities, allowing for comprehensive analysis through simultaneous imaging techniques. This combination enhances the depth of information gathered from studies, facilitating more accurate assessments of biological processes in pre-clinical research settings.</p></p>
