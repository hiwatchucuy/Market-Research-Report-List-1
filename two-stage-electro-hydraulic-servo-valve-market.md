<p><p>The Two-stage Electro-hydraulic Servo Valve market includes three main types: Nozzle Flapper Valve, Jet Action Valve, and Direct Drive Valve. The Nozzle Flapper Valve uses a flapper mechanism to control hydraulic fluid flow and is known for high sensitivity. Jet Action Valves utilize a jet of fluid to create a force that adjusts the valve's position, offering rapid response. Direct Drive Valves employ a direct actuation system, enhancing precision and reducing mechanical errors, making them suitable for high-performance applications.</p></p>
