<p><p>The pigmentation disorder treatment market includes various conditions such as melasma, vitiligo, albinism, and post-inflammatory hyperpigmentation. Melasma is characterized by brown patches on the skin, often due to hormonal changes. Vitiligo involves the loss of skin color in patches, resulting from autoimmune responses. Albinism is a genetic condition leading to little or no pigment across the skin, hair, and eyes. Post-inflammatory hyperpigmentation occurs after skin injury, causing dark spots. Each condition requires targeted therapies for effective management.</p></p>
