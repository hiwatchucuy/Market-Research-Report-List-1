<p><p>The medical otoscope market is categorized into two main types: straight otoscopes and otoscopes with speculum attachments. Straight otoscopes are designed for direct visualization of the ear canal and tympanic membrane, offering simplicity and ease of use. In contrast, otoscopes with speculum come with interchangeable tips that allow for better access and examination of varying ear canal sizes. This versatility enhances diagnostic capabilities in clinical settings, catering to diverse patient needs and improving overall otologic assessments.</p></p>
