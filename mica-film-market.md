<p><p>The mica film market is categorized primarily into two types: Muscovite and Phlogopite. Muscovite mica film is known for its excellent thermal and electrical insulation properties, making it suitable for a wide range of applications in electronics and power generation. In contrast, Phlogopite mica film offers superior thermal stability and is commonly used in high-temperature applications, such as in the automotive and aerospace industries. Both types are valued for their lightweight, flexible nature and are essential in various industrial applications.</p></p>
