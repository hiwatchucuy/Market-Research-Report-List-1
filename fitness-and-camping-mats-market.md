<p><p>The fitness and camping mats market comprises various materials, each offering distinct features. XLPE (cross-linked polyethylene) is known for its durability and lightweight properties, while NXLPE (non-cross-linked polyethylene) offers flexibility and cushioning. EVA (ethylene-vinyl acetate) provides excellent shock absorption and comfort, whereas PVC (polyvinyl chloride) is valued for its waterproofness and affordability. Rubbers, including NBR (nitrile butadiene rubber) and natural rubber, enhance grip and cushioning. Other natural materials, like fibers and cork, promote eco-friendliness and sustainability in mat production.</p></p>
