<p><p>The diammonium phosphate (DAP) fertilizer market can be segmented into two main types: crystalline and granular. Crystalline DAP features a uniform, fine texture ideal for precision application and rapid soil absorption, making it suitable for high-efficiency farming practices. In contrast, granular DAP offers a coarser texture that facilitates easier handling and spreading, making it popular for bulk usage in larger agricultural settings. Both forms provide essential nutrients, but their applications vary based on soil conditions and farming techniques.</p></p>
