<p><p>The aluminum beverage cans market consists of two primary types: three-piece cans and two-piece cans. Three-piece cans are constructed from three separate sections—two cylindrical bodies and a top—commonly used for products requiring a sturdy structure. In contrast, two-piece cans are made from a single aluminum sheet, formed into a body with an integrated top, offering a sleeker design and reduced weight. Both types are widely used for beverages, balancing durability, aesthetics, and production efficiency.</p></p>
