<p><strong>The Molecular Modelling Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Molecular Mechanics Approach</li><li>Quantum Chemistry Approach</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The molecular modeling market is categorized into two primary types: the molecular mechanics approach and the quantum chemistry approach. The molecular mechanics approach utilizes classical physics to simulate molecular interactions, focusing on the forces between atoms to predict molecular behavior. In contrast, the quantum chemistry approach employs quantum mechanical principles to calculate electronic structures and interactions, providing more precise insights into molecular properties. Both approaches are essential for drug design, materials science, and understanding biochemical processes, driving advancements in various industries.</p></p>
