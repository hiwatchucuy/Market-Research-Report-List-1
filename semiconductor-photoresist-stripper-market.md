<p><strong>The Semiconductor Photoresist Stripper Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positive Photoresist Stripper</li><li>Negative Photoresist Stripper</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor photoresist stripper market is divided into two primary types: positive photoresist strippers and negative photoresist strippers. Positive photoresist strippers are designed to remove photoresist layers that become soluble during exposure to light, facilitating pattern development on semiconductor wafers. Conversely, negative photoresist strippers target photoresists that harden upon exposure, requiring chemical agents to dissolve the unexposed areas. Both types are crucial for clean and efficient semiconductor manufacturing, ensuring precise pattern transfer and optimal device performance.</p></p>
