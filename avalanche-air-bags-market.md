<p><p>Avalanche air bags are essential safety devices used in backcountry skiing and snowboarding to mitigate the risks of avalanches. Mono avalanche air bags feature a single large compartment that inflates to help keep the skier on the surface of the snow during an avalanche. In contrast, dual avalanche air bags comprise two separate compartments, enhancing stability and control in turbulent conditions. Both types are designed to prevent burial and increase survival chances, catering to various user preferences and terrain challenges.</p></p>
