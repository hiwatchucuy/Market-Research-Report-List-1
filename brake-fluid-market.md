<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Brake Fluid Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>DOT 3</li><li>DOT 4</li><li>DOT 5</li><li>DOT 5.1</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The brake fluid market is primarily categorized into DOT 3, DOT 4, DOT 5, and DOT 5.1 types. DOT 3 is glycol-based, suitable for most vehicles, while DOT 4 offers higher boiling points, making it ideal for performance applications. DOT 5 is silicone-based and not compatible with glycol fluids, suitable for specific racing and military uses. DOT 5.1, also glycol-based, provides enhanced performance similar to DOT 4 but with better high-temperature resistance, appealing to a wide range of vehicles.</p></p>
