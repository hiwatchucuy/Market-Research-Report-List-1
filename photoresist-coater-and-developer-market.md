<p><p>Photoresist coaters and developers are essential tools in the semiconductor manufacturing process, used for applying photoresist materials and developing the patterns required for circuit fabrication. The coater deposits a thin layer of photoresist on the substrate, while the developer is used to wash away the exposed or unexposed portions of this material, depending on the type of photoresist used. </p><p>The Photoresist Coater and Developer Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.8% during the forecast period, driven by the increasing demand for advanced semiconductor devices, particularly in sectors like consumer electronics, automotive, and telecommunications. The evolution towards smaller, more efficient chips necessitates more sophisticated photolithography processes, further boosting the need for high-precision coaters and developers. </p><p>Recent trends include the rise of extreme ultraviolet (EUV) lithography and the greater adoption of integrated circuit solutions. Additionally, the focus on miniaturization and the development of 5G technology and IoT devices are propelling market growth.
