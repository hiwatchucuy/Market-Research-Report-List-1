<p><p>The automotive battery management systems (BMS) market is categorized into three main types: Li-ion batteries, Ni-MH batteries, and lead-acid batteries. Li-ion batteries dominate due to their high energy density, lightweight design, and longer life, making them ideal for electric vehicles. Ni-MH batteries, while less efficient, are widely used in hybrid vehicles due to their reliability and safety. Lead-acid batteries, known for their cost-effectiveness and robustness, are primarily employed in conventional vehicles, providing reliable starting power despite lesser energy density.</p></p>
