<p><strong>The Denture Adhesives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Restoration Bonding</li><li>Indirect Restoration Bonding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The denture adhesives market can be categorized into two main types: direct restoration bonding and indirect restoration bonding. Direct restoration bonding involves adhesives applied directly to the denture base or the tissue, providing immediate adhesion and stability for removable dentures. In contrast, indirect restoration bonding entails the use of custom-made connectors or frameworks that are created outside the mouth, promoting better fit and comfort. Both methods aim to enhance the retention and functionality of dentures for users.</p></p>
