<p><p>The Multilayer Ceramic Capacitor (MLCC) market is divided into two main types: Class 1 and Class 2. Class 1 capacitors offer stable capacitance with minimal temperature and voltage dependence, making them ideal for high-frequency and timing applications. In contrast, Class 2 capacitors provide higher capacitance values but have a greater variation in capacitance with temperature and voltage changes, which makes them suitable for decoupling and filtering applications in consumer electronics. Each class serves distinct market demands based on performance requirements.</p></p>
