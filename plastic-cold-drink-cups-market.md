<p><p>The plastic cold drink cups market primarily comprises four types: PET, PLA, PP, and others. PET (polyethylene terephthalate) cups are popular for their clarity and recyclability, ideal for beverages. PLA (polylactic acid) cups are biodegradable, made from renewable resources, appealing for eco-conscious consumers. PP (polypropylene) cups are durable and resistant to high temperatures, often used for hot beverages as well. The "others" category includes various materials such as PVC or biodegradable composites, catering to niche markets and varying consumer preferences.</p></p>
