<p><p>The Eye Viscoelastic Agent market consists primarily of Sodium Hyaluronate and Hydroxypropylmethylcellulose, both of which are used in ophthalmic surgeries. Sodium Hyaluronate is a naturally occurring polymer that provides lubrication and increases viscosity, enhancing surgical precision and protecting ocular tissues. Hydroxypropylmethylcellulose, a synthetic polymer, offers similar viscoelastic properties and is beneficial for maintaining intraocular pressure during procedures. Both agents are essential in cataract surgery, retinal procedures, and other eye surgeries, ensuring safer and more effective outcomes.</p></p>
