<p><p>The Fibre Drum Packaging Market is segmented into three main types based on closure materials: metal closure, plastic closure, and fiber closure. Metal closures provide a secure seal and are often used for hazardous materials, ensuring tamper resistance and durability. Plastic closures are lightweight and offer moisture resistance, making them suitable for a variety of products. Fiber closures, made from the same material as the drum, provide an eco-friendly option, maintaining structural integrity while minimizing environmental impact.</p></p>
