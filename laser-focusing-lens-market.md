<p><ul><li>Plano-convex Lens</li><li>Meniscus Lens</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The laser focusing lens market includes two primary types: plano-convex lenses and meniscus lenses. Plano-convex lenses are characterized by one flat surface and one convex surface, optimizing light convergence for applications like laser beam focusing. Meniscus lenses, which feature one concave and one convex surface, effectively minimize optical aberrations, enhancing image quality. Both lens types are crucial in industries such as telecommunications, medical devices, and manufacturing, shaping the performance and efficiency of laser systems across various applications.</p></p>
