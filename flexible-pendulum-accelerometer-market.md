<p><strong>The Flexible Pendulum Accelerometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Piezoelectric Type</li><li>Piezoresistive Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flexible pendulum accelerometer market includes two main types: piezoelectric and piezoresistive. Piezoelectric accelerometers generate electrical charges in response to mechanical stress, making them ideal for high-frequency measurements. They are commonly used in seismic monitoring and structural analysis. Piezoresistive accelerometers, on the other hand, change their electrical resistance with applied acceleration, offering high sensitivity and stability for low-frequency applications. Both types cater to diverse industries, including automotive, aerospace, and consumer electronics, enhancing motion detection and monitoring capabilities.</p></p>
