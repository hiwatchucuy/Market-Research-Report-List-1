<p><ul><li>Duct Filters</li><li>Non-Duct Filters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The kitchen filters market comprises two main types: duct filters and non-duct filters. Duct filters are integrated into ventilation systems, capturing airborne particles and odors before they enter the ductwork, making them effective for whole kitchen environments. In contrast, non-duct filters operate independently, using standalone units to purify air around specific cooking areas. Both types play a crucial role in improving indoor air quality, enhancing comfort, and ensuring safety by mitigating smoke, grease, and unpleasant smells generated during cooking.</p></p>
