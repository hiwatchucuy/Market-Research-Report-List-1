<p><strong>The CCD Camera Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Area Scan Camera</li><li>Line Scan Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CCD camera market is primarily divided into area scan cameras and line scan cameras. Area scan cameras capture entire images at once, making them suitable for applications like industrial inspection and medical imaging where detailed image capture is essential. In contrast, line scan cameras record one line of pixels at a time, ideal for high-speed applications such as web inspection and barcode scanning. Each type caters to specific imaging needs, impacting their use in various industries.</p></p>
